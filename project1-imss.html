IMSS Predictive Cases

This project focuses on building a predictive model to forecast morbidity trends within the Mexican Social Security Institute (IMSS). Using historical structured datasets, we developed a neural network with Keras capable of projecting the number of disease-related cases for the years 2025 and 2026. The model incorporates temporal patterns, and its results are fully visualized through a dynamic Power BI dashboard.

By interacting with the dashboard (page 2, using the Ctrl key), stakeholders can analyze the behavior of various IMSS assets, observing where increases or decreases are projected in medical service areas and investments.

Beyond prediction, the analysis enabled us to generate strategic recommendations for IMSS decision-makers, highlighting areas of opportunity, operational risks, and potential shifts in resource allocation. This end-to-end approach—from modeling to storytelling delivers data-driven insights tailored for public health management.

Project Highlights

  • Goal: Predict disease case patterns based on historical IMSS data.
  • Tech Stack: Python (Pandas, Keras), Power BI, Jupyter.
  • Outcome: Live dashboards for analysis and forecasting.
