Professional Experience at Danone

During my tenure at Danone, I developed and deployed business intelligence solutions using Power BI to support cybersecurity teams and SAP operations. These dashboards offered real-time insights and streamlined complex workflows into clear, user-friendly visuals for both technical and executive audiences.

Cybersecurity Risk Monitoring

This dashboard identifies and categorizes potential risks within Danone’s critical applications. Designed with a radar chart to visualize compliance gaps, it enables product owners to interpret cybersecurity exposure across domains such as data protection, resilience, and OT management. The risk scoring is calculated from multiple control systems and automatically aggregates IT standard references for further governance alignment.

Note: Certain sections of this dashboard have been intentionally blurred to comply with Danone’s internal data confidentiality policies.

SAP Incident Automation Dashboard

I created an automated incident tracker for the SAP team that visualizes real-time data on pending, closed, and resolved tickets. It segments incidents by module, highlights workload distribution, and simplifies prioritization for team leaders. This has allowed for faster resolutions and more informed decision-making in operational support.
